How to Play

  1. You will be presented with a riddle and some answers

  2. When you choose an answer, click on that box

  3. Choose the right answer, and a path appears

  4. Choose the wrong one... and something else appears

  5. If a path appears, follow the path to the next question

  6. We believe in you!

  7. Let's Start
